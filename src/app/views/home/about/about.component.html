about us.

We at Scentaholics vow to take you on a remarkable fragrance journey. Through our fragrance reviews, we aim to educate & introduce you to the very best luxury scents & hidden gems the world has to offer. We will take you from smelling safe & upgrade you to distinctive crowd pleasing scents.

Scentaholics was born from a deep passion and love for fine fragrances. We are based in Johannesburg where the journey began. All our fragrances are 100% authentic and genuine. We import exceptional & carefully selected scents and provide them to our Scentaholic family at affordable prices.

We take customer service seriously and strive to ensure that your purchase is as smooth as your fragrance.
